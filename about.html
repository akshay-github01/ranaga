रंगवर्धन....व्हीजेटीआयचं मराठी मन!


Rangawardhan is a Marathi cultural society running under VJTI Social Group which has enormous impacton cultural atmosphere of VJTI. Rangawardhan is nothing but basically Central Platform for all the Students who have an artist hidden in them. 17 years ago, few art loving students and professors came together with the aim of preservation of Marathi Culture in the college and established Rangawardhan which today hastransformed into cultural fest, popular not only in Mumbai but all over Maharashtra !
